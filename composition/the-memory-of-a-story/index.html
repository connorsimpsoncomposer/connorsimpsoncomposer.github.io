the Memory of a Story

The Memory of a Story was written in Spring, 2022.

Program Notes:

The Memory of a Story is not about any specific story, but rather the way in which we share our stories. Over time each story we tell changes. We learn which parts to exaggerate and what will get a laugh. We remember not just the story itself but the act of telling the story. With each telling, these stories become not just records of the past but new moments of connection.

Connor Simpson is a composer based in State College, Pennsylvania. His work Panic was selected through juried review for the New York City Electroacoustic Music Festival. Connor has also participated in a variety of international music festivals such as soundSCAPE, Longy’s Divergent Studio, and Charlotte New Music Festival. Connor has written in a variety of genres and styles in acoustic and electronic composition. His works include who knows if the moon’s a balloon for Loadbang, ill for quadraphonic playback, and Prancer for Wind Ensemble. Connor is pursuing his Bachelor of Music with Dr. Baljinder Sekhon and Dr. Steven Rice at Penn State University.
